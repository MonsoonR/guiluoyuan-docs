# About Us

## Introduction

GuiLuoYuan, established in 2018 through the reorganization of its predecessor organization. We provide support for "[Community Chat Convention](../docs/treaty/community_communication_covenant)" and community rule writing for many public communities, and offer multiple community game servers including Minecraft, Terraria, and more.

We are dedicated to integrating all those who love life, uphold integrity, and are friendly and kind. Together with all community members, we strive to build a joyful and unforgettable interest community. We aim to create a warm, harmonious, loving, wonderful, and diverse community, establishing one with distinct themes and genuine content. We encourage content that is loving, interesting, and useful to thrive within the community.
